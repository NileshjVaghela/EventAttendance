# Attendee User Manual — Event Attendance System

## Overview

As an attendee, you use your **mobile phone browser** to:
1. Check in at the registration desk
2. Mark attendance at breakout sessions

**No app installation required.**

---

## Part 1: Event Check-in

### Step 1: Scan the QR Code

- At the registration desk, scan the displayed QR code with your phone camera
- It opens a web page in your browser

### Step 2: Enter Your Sequence Number

- Enter your **3-4 digit sequence number** (provided during registration)
- Tap **Continue**

### Step 3: Enter OTP

- A 6-digit OTP is sent to your **registered email**
- Check your inbox (and spam folder)
- Enter the OTP and tap **Verify**

### Step 4: Show Your Phone to Desk Staff

After verification, your phone displays your name, designation, and company in large text. Hold it up for desk staff to confirm.

---

## Part 2: Session Attendance

### Step 1: Scan the Session QR Code

- Each session has a QR code on the stage/screen
- Scan it with your phone

### Step 2: Enter Your Sequence Number

- Enter your sequence number (same one used during check-in)
- Tap **Mark Attendance**

### Step 3: Confirmation

- You'll see: **"Attendance recorded for [Session Name]"**

---

## Important Notes

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| No OTP for sessions | Session attendance only needs sequence number |
| Session timing | QR only works while session is active |
| Duplicate scan | Shows "Already recorded" — no harm done |
| OTP expiry | Valid for 10 minutes. Max 5 OTPs total |
| OTP not arriving | Check spam folder. Wait 30 seconds. Contact desk staff if persistent |
| Browser support | iOS Safari, Android Chrome (latest versions) |

---

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I find my sequence number?**
A: Provided during registration (email confirmation or printed badge). Ask desk staff if unsure.

**Q: Can I check in on someone else's behalf?**
A: No. OTP is sent to the registered email only.

**Q: What if the session QR says "Session not active"?**
A: The session hasn't started yet or has ended. Check the schedule.
